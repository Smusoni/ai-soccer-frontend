What is Ball Knowledge?

Ball Knowledge is your personal soccer assistant. Upload a training clip, tell us what skill you’re working on, and get a clear, coach-style report with focus areas and suggested drills so you know exactly what to work on next.

  • Built for players who want to get better between training sessions
  • Simple: record on your phone, upload, and get feedback
  • No fancy camera or GPS required

Privacy & Data

Your clips and reports stay tied to your account. We use them only to generate your analysis and to help improve the quality of feedback over time.

  • We don’t sell your data
  • You can delete reports from your Library anytime
  • Use short clips that you’re comfortable sharing

Feedback & Bug Reports

If something breaks or you have ideas for new features, I’d love to hear it. During this early version, your feedback helps shape where Ball Knowledge goes next.
